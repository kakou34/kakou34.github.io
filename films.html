My Favourite Movies

I don't have a specific type of movies that I prefer. However, I do not watch horror movies. I find them pointless. On the other hand, I do enjoy adventure and sci-fi movies. Below this section, you will find a list of my top 5 movies.

Pirates of The Caribbean

Pirates of the Caribbean (start: 2003) is a series of five fantasy swashbuckler films produced by Jerry Bruckheimer and based on Walt Disney's eponymous theme park ride. Directors of the series include Gore Verbinski, Rob Marshall and Joachim Rønning and Espen Sandberg.

  • The Curse Of The Black Pearl
  • Dead Man's Chest
  • At World's End
  • On Stranger Tides
  • Dead Men Tell No Tales

Divergent

The Before Trilogy

On his way to Vienna, American Jesse meets Celine, a student returning to Paris. After long conversations forge a surprising connection between them, Jesse convinces Celine to get off the train with him in Vienna. Since his flight to the U.S. departs the next morning and he has no money for lodging, they wander the city together, taking in the experiences of Vienna and each other. As the night progresses, their bond makes separating in the morning a difficult choice
